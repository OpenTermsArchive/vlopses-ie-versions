Stripchat model rules set clear content standards, ensure safety and compliance, and help maintain a respectful, high-quality platform for all users. These Community Guidelines are designed to ensure the best experience for everyone, as well as to maintain the legality, safety, and quality of all content. By using the Website, you agree to these guidelines and our [Terms of Use](https://stripchat.com/terms).

**Consent & Verification**

*   We do not allow anyone to upload pictures or videos of a person without that person’s consent and without that person being registered and verified on our Website.
*   We do not allow models to broadcast unless they have successfully passed our KYC process on the account they are broadcasting from. This applies to every person appearing in the broadcast,  meaning that only individuals who are registered and verified on the specific account may appear on that account's broadcasts and content. Verification on other accounts does not grant permission to appear on all accounts.
*   If you are streaming from a public place, take appropriate measures to ensure that unregistered persons do not appear on your stream. If there is a high risk that non-registered persons might appear and you cannot ensure otherwise, then we recommend that you stop the stream. This includes showing non-registered persons on your phone, persons visible in the background such as individuals on streets, in public venues, or appearing on TV screens or other screen displays while streaming.
*   We will take action against accounts if we have sufficient reason to believe they are currently using or have previously used non-authentic or tampered documents.
*   We will terminate any person’s account and submit a report to the relevant authorities if we have sufficient reason to believe they are participating in any way in human trafficking.
*   In Group accounts, the Account Holder must be present on camera during every stream.

**Minor Safety**

*   All models and users must be at least 18 years of age.
*   The sexualization of minors is strictly prohibited in any form or medium. This includes, but is not limited to, role-play and pretending to be underage.

**Self-Harm**

*   We do not allow any content that promotes self-harm.

**Dangerous & Regulated Goods**

*   We prohibit any unsafe activities, including the use of dangerous tools.
*   We do not allow the sharing of any illegal or regulated products or services, such as illegal drugs, on our Website.

**Violent & Graphic Content**

*   We prohibit showing certain bodily fluids as they are deemed disturbing to most of our users; this includes blood and human excrement.
*   We also do not allow the depiction of violence on our Website in any form.

**Harassment & Hate Speech**

*   We prohibit the use of hateful or hurtful language, and we prohibit all harassment of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to, insults, body shaming, wishing harm or death on others, threats, blackmail, coercion, or repeated abusive behavior.
*   We understand that conversations can sometimes feel provoking or emotionally charged. However, we strongly encourage you to report any incident rather than retaliate, as responding with abuse is also a violation of these guidelines.

**Animal Welfare**

*   We do not allow the depiction of bestiality (sexual activity between a human and an animal) on our Website.
*   We also do not allow physical interaction with animals while the model is performing a sexual act.

**Protection of Privacy**

*   We do not allow any unauthorized exposure of personal private information. This includes, but is not limited to, another person's full name, address, contact details, or any other information sufficient to identify them.
*   We do not allow arranging in-person meetings through our platform.
*   We do not allow any recording by any party during Cam2Cam shows without the consent of all participants.

**Spam & Unwanted Advertisement**

*   We review the Website 24/7 to ensure that it remains free of spam and is not being used as a gateway to drive users away from the Website. This includes, but is not limited to, repeatedly sharing ways of contact leading outside our platform, sharing malicious links, offering or arranging shows outside our platform, advertising other live-streaming platforms, content subscription services, or any case in which it is sufficiently clear that the account’s primary purpose is to lead users outside our platform. 

**Scams & Fraud**

Any dishonest or misleading behavior on the Website is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Fake tipping;
*   Using pre-recorded content in broadcasts;
*   Asking for, suggesting, or accepting payment methods other than those offered by the Website;
*   Using fake toys;
*   Pretending to be a member of our staff.

**Performance Quality & User Satisfaction**

We work alongside our models to ensure that all performances are highly satisfying for our users. We do not allow:

*   Not performing items from your public or private tip menus; You have full control over what you add to your public and private tip menus. Only add items you are confident you can perform.
*   Not performing a show after agreeing to it; You are not obliged to agree to any request you do not want to perform.
*   Not performing a completed goal, or updating your goals right before completion on purpose.
*   Using misleading titles for your ticket and/or group shows
*   Depicting unconscious persons or broadcasting while sleeping;
*   Begging for tips to cover personal expenses;
*   Broadcasting from two or more accounts at the same time;
*   Advertising a room in other models’ rooms;
*   Advertising studios on our Website;
*   Discussing, requesting, or offering prohibited shows or topics, including incest, bestiality, shows related to urination, menstruation, scat, and any other show or topic that is explicitly prohibited under this article;
*   Broadcasting Private or Exclusive Private shows on other platforms publicly for free.

**Account Access & Security**

*   Each registered user or model on our Website is entitled to access only their own account using the credentials provided during registration. Under no circumstances should anyone seek to access, use, or interfere with another person’s account.

**Prohibition of Content Use**

*   Users are expressly prohibited from copying, reproducing, distributing, publishing, or otherwise engaging in any unauthorized use or exploitation of any content from our Website on any third-party websites.

Violation of these guidelines may result in the temporary suspension or permanent termination of account access and any tokens being withheld. We reserve the right to remove any content that breaches our Community Guidelines. For a detailed understanding of our rules and the corresponding penalties, please refer to our [Rules & Penalties](https://support.stripchat.com/hc/en-us/articles/22536961950097-Rules-Penalties).  
  
We reserve the right to modify, amend, or update these guidelines at our sole discretion and at any time, as deemed necessary.  
  
Thank you for helping us uphold these standards and for contributing to a welcoming and safe environment for everyone.

Stripchat operates under a set of strict guidelines to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all models involved. These rules are put in place to protect the rights and well-being of models while also adhering to legal requirements and industry standards.

It is essential for studios to familiarize themselves with the following rules and categories as part of their responsibility to maintain a professional and compliant working environment.

#### **Legal Age Verification**

\- Studios must verify the legal age of all models before engaging in any activities.

\- Models must provide valid identification to verify their age and legal eligibility to Stripchat.

#### **Coercion and Abuse**

\- Models should not be coerced or pressured into activities without their free will and/or activities that may harm their well-being.

\- Stripchat reserves the right to permanently block any Studios that abuse or coerce models.  

\- Studios must maintain a professional and respectful working environment, free from harassment, discrimination, or any form of abusive behavior towards models.

\- If a model reports a studio for abuse and coercion, the company reserves the right to detach the said model without any further notice.

#### **Working Place Conditions**

– The studio, providing physical spaces for models to conduct live streaming, is responsible for maintaining safe and comfortable working conditions in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

This responsibility includes, but is not limited to, adhering to international labor standards on privacy, cubicle dimensions, lighting, working hours, shift schedules, break periods, sanitation, and access to water and restroom facilities.

– The studio must address any additional occupational health and safety requirements to protect the well-being of all models.

#### **Image Control and Consent**

\- Models have the right to control their own content and decide how their content is used and distributed.

\- Stripchat reserves the right to remove any content reported by a model depicting it as non-consensual.

#### **Legal Compliance**

\- Studios must operate within the legal frameworks of the jurisdictions they operate in, adhering to all relevant laws and regulations.

\- Studios must provide valid and correct "know-your-customer" information to Stripchat.

\- The engagement in any illegal activities is strictly prohibited.

\- Studios must comply with all rules and regulations applicable to the adult industry, including  Mastercard's and Visa's Security Rules and Procedures Manual.

\- Studios should at all times pay the corresponding commission owed to the model.

\- Studios should provide clear and transparent communication to models regarding expectations, compensation, and any potential risks involved.

\- Detailed contracts should be established, outlining the rights and responsibilities of both parties.

\- Studios must provide evidence of contractual relationships with models upon request for legal purposes.

#### **Complaint Reporting and Action Plan**

\- Studios should have a robust reporting system in place for models to report any abuse, harassment, or misconduct.

\- Prompt and appropriate action must be taken to address any reported issues, including investigating allegations and taking necessary disciplinary actions.

\- Studios should immediately inform Stripchat of the existence of such a complaint.

Studios agree to adhere and must ensure that the models are aware about the following prohibitions:

\- Discussion, involvement, or portrayal of illegal subjects, minors, incest, bestiality, chat, or actual display of violence, or any unsafe and extreme activities which include, among others, blood, torture, pain, asphyxiation is strictly prohibited.

#### **Meeting and Prostitution**

\- Studios are prohibited from promoting or facilitating in any way prostitution, solicitation of prostitution, human trafficking, or sex trafficking.

\- In addition, Studios must ensure that Models are not arranging illegal in-person meetings involving sexual activities for hire.

#### **Compliance and Communication**

\- Studios must ensure that broadcasting locations comply with rules and regulations.

\- Models may only appear on the website with the final approval of the Operator in writing.

It is of utmost importance for studios to comply with these rules and regulations to maintain a professional and ethical working environment. Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in severe consequences, including termination of account with Stripchat.

It is the responsibility of studios to stay updated with any modifications made to these rules, as they are subject to change. By prioritizing the well-being and rights of models, studios contribute to the creation of a safe and respectful community within the industry.
