How we keep reviews trustworthy and useful?

To ensure only real customers post reviews, we have submission requirements. Customers who have purchased items on Temu are eligible to submit ratings and reviews for the items they have purchased.

Before posting a review, we check if it meets our [Community Guidelines](https://www.temu.com/community-guidelines.html).

We listen to our customers. If you think a review violates our guidelines, you can report the review and we'll investigate it.

How we calculate overall star ratings?

The overall star rating reflects the average of all valid ratings submitted by customers. A star rating may be excluded from the average if its review violates Temu's Review guidelines.

What are our review guidelines?

When you write reviews about Temu or items sold on Temu, you should:

1\. Be truthful.

Your review, including words, photos, and other materials, should be about your own experience on Temu or about a product sold on Temu. Your review should not mislead viewers about any feature, function, or quality of a product.

2\. Be respectful.

Your review should not contain anything that is obscene, libelous, hateful, violent, offensive, illegal, sexually explicit, or otherwise inappropriate.

3\. Respect intellectual property rights.

Your review should not contain any material that you do not have permission to use or infringes on any intellectual property of any third party.

4\. Use product as intended.

Your review should be about using a product as intended by the manufacturer. Your review should not depict a product being used in an unintended manner, especially in any unsafe or illegal manner.

5\. Be mindful of privacy.

Your review should not contain personal information of your own or a third party, including phone numbers, mailing addresses, email addresses, etc.

6\. Do not mention competitors.

Your review should not compare products or services of other competitors by name.

7\. Do not mention promotional codes.

Your review should not contain any information about promotional codes or invitation codes.

For more details, please visit our [Community Guidelines](https://www.temu.com/community-guidelines.html).

How long are reviews displayed?

As long as the review meets the review guidelines and [Community Guidelines](https://www.temu.com/community-guidelines.html), and the item is currently available, the review will be displayed.

Are sponsored or influenced reviews allowed on Temu?

Traders are not allowed to sponsor or influence reviews posted on Temu.

Temu Community Guidelines
=========================

*   English
_|_*   Gaeilge

We have these Community Guidelines to ensure that all users can enjoy a safe, respectful and positive experience on Temu. These Community Guidelines apply to all Temu users and are part of the Temu [Terms of Use](https://www.temu.com/terms-of-use.html).

These rules cover what is expected when you use our applications, products, services and websites (“Services”), including when you:

*   Interact with the platform or other Temu users.
    

*   Share photos, videos, links or text.
    

*   Post reviews, ratings or “helpful” votes.
    

*   Create lists (including wishlists), profile pages or customized products.
    

*   Report violations.
    

By using our services, you agree to follow these rules to keep the community safe.

**1\. User Safety**


-----------------------

#### 

**1.1. Illegal or Harmful Activities**

You must not engage in any illegal or harmful conduct when using our Services. That includes:

*   Engaging, promoting or encouraging others to engage in violence or any illegal activity, including child or animal abuse, cyber violence, illegal drug use, or the use of alcohol, drugs or other restricted substance by minors or undocumented individuals.
    

*   Advocating or threatening physical or financial harm to yourself or others, including acts of terrorism, self-harm.
    

*   Making jokes or sarcastic comments about causing harm.
    

*   Engaging in manipulative or deceptive conduct, including coordinating, amplifying or promoting misinformation, disinformation or propaganda intended to mislead users or manipulate public perception.
    

*   Offering fraudulent goods, services, promotions, or schemes, including “get-rich-quick” or pyramid schemes.
    

*   Encouraging the dangerous, unsafe or offensive misuse of a product.
    

#### 

**1.2. Discrimination, Hate Speech, Harassment and Defamation**

Temu is an inclusive space for everyone. We don’t allow discriminatory, hateful, harassing, or defamatory content. We strictly prohibit:

*   Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, age, disability, or other personal traits.
    

*   Promotions of groups that spread hate speech.
    

*   Harassment, intimidation, stalking, bullying or threats.
    

*   Libel, defamation or inflammatory content.
    

#### 

**1.3 Other Offensive or Harmful Content**

To ensure a respectful and inclusive environment, we don’t allow content that is offensive, abusive or otherwise harmful. This includes:

*   Offensive, inappropriate or objectionable content, including swearing, name-calling or abusive language (even if it includes symbols or is translated into another language), as well as gory, violent, disgusting, terrifying or otherwise offensive imagery or material.
    

*   Attempts to drown out or silence other users.
    

*   Unrelated content (such as politics, terrorism, religion or war) that could be harmful, including through having negative effects on civic discourse, elections or public health or security.
    

#### 

**1.4. Sexual Content**

We do not allow descriptive or graphic depictions of sexual activity, especially when they objectify or exploit others. Specifically, we do not allow any content:

*   Depicting minors in any sexualized or suggestive manner, including luring, grooming, child nudity or enticing minors into sexual activity.
    

*   Describing or displaying pornographic, vulgar, or nude content that is unrelated to the product.
    

*   That could promote human trafficking or exploitation, including content that implies control, coercion, or abuse for personal or commercial gain.
    

*   Featuring individuals without their consent, such as voyeuristic or shaming material.
    

Sexual content may be allowed only if it is educational, legal, and respectful, or contains descriptions, illustrations and reviews directly related to products sold on Temu. We recognize that cultural norms and perceptions of clothing and nudity vary across countries and regions, and these differences are considered during the content moderation process.

**2\. Content Integrity and Authenticity**


----------------------------------------------

#### 

**2.1. Misleading, Deceptive and AI-generated Content**

We don’t allow anyone to post misleading or deceptive content. This means that you must:

*   Not post misleading content about characteristics, quality or performance of products.
    

*   Use clear, high-resolution images and videos that accurately show the product and avoid misleading edits.
    

*   Not exaggerate sustainability or any sustainability-related benefits of products.
    

*   Not post AI-generated content where it is created or heavily edited by AI in a way that misleads or intends to deceive users.
    

#### 

**2.2. Honest and Safe Reviews**

Genuine reviews help users make informed decisions. Misleading reviews damage trust. We expect you to only share reviews that honestly reflect your experience. Do not exaggerate or mislead.

*   Only review products you have used properly (as intended by the manufacturer).
    

*   Do not show or describe a product being used in an unintended way, especially in any unsafe or illegal way.
    

*   Do not post false or misleading reviews that unfairly disparage or exaggerate a product, threaten others, or otherwise misrepresent the actual user experience.
    

If you ask other users to post reviews, do not pressure them to be positive or offer rewards. That means you must not:

*   Try to influence others to leave a positive rating or review.
    

*   Offer, request, or accept compensation of any kind for creating, editing, or posting content. If you have a personal or financial connection to a merchandise partner, you must clearly disclose it when post content about that merchandise partner.
    

#### 

**2.3. Marketing Content and External links**

To create an authentic and trustful community for everyone, Temu strictly prohibits unauthorized promotions and advertisements, content posted by people with financial interests in the content, or any other conflicts of interest, and links to external businesses or services. We do not allow content that:

*   Promotes an external company, services, website, author, advertisement or special offer (including any political content).
    

*   Describes people’s own products or services, or products or services offered by their friends, relatives, employers, business associates, or competitors.
    

*   Includes links to external sites, including URLs containing referrer tags or affiliate codes.
    

#### 

**2.4. Spam**

To maintain an authentic and enjoyable experience for everyone, Temu prohibits spam and irrelevant content. You must keep your posts relevant and helpful, and must not spam users. That means you do not post:

*   Spam or repetitive content, including posts or comments that add no meaningful value or are repeatedly copied.
    

*   Distracting, nonsensical or irrelevant content that is unrelated to products or services.
    

*   Meaningless text, punctuation or symbols, such as random text or characters, blank or solid-color images, or repeated emojis or symbols.
    

**3\. Privacy and Intellectual Property**


---------------------------------------------

#### 

**3.1. Personal Information**

Protecting privacy is essential. Sharing personal data can put people at risk. You must ensure you do not post personal information about yourself or others, and do not invade the privacy of others. This includes, but is not limited to, posting:

*   Phone numbers
    

*   Residential addresses
    

*   Email and postal addresses
    

*   License plates
    

*   Order numbers
    

*   Tax numbers
    

*   Personal social media account
    

*   Any other information revealing personal identification
    

#### 

**3.2. Respect Intellectual Property**

Temu values creativity and ownership. You must respect the rights of others and only post content or customize products that you have permission to use. Do not:

*   Copy or use someone else’s work without permission.
    

*   Pretend to be another individual or organization.
    

*   Post content featuring another person without their consent, including deepfakes.
    

*   Otherwise infringe on anyone’s intellectual property or other personal or legal rights.
    

Check out the Temu [Intellectual Property Policy](https://www.temu.com/intellectual-property-policy.html) for more information.
